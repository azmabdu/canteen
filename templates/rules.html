Tashkent International School Canteen
Rules

1. Food waste must be disposed of properly in the trash cans provided.

2. No outside food or drinks are allowed in the canteen.

3. Food must be consumed at designated tables and not taken out of the canteen.

4. No food or drinks are allowed to be taken away from the canteen without permission.

5. Students are responsible for cleaning their own table after finishing their meal.
